About Us

Welcome! We are Stargazers, a relaxed and casual RotMG guild. Our goal is to build a close community that is friendly and helpful for everyone - both guild members and other players. We want to set an example through kind and thoughtful actions on the realm. We play on USWest2 and we are currently recruiting!

Discord

We have a public discord that everyone can join. However, please read the read-me when you first join the server. The permanent link is: https://discord.gg/0yTZ4SUckuugD4Cc

Guild Requirements:

1) You must not continuously show rude or hurtful behavior on discord or in game. This includes hacking, scamming, begging, or just being extremely annoying in general.
2) You must have discord and join the guild server. Voice chat is highly recommended.
3) You must be active at least for one hour each week. Please note that this is the bare minimum. If you can, one hour a day is optimal.
4) Tomb/Shatters experience is highly recommended. If you do not know how to complete tombs or shatters, you must be willing to learn.

This is the application form. Please fill it out honestly. If you are accepted into the guild, you will be an initiate for a trial period of one week. During this week, we will see how well you interact with the guild. If you are a good fit for the guild, you will be promoted to member, and you will only be kicked if you go inactive for too long or break a rule more than once.
